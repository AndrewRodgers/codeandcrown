Hello there! I’m Andrew.

I’ve been working for over 15 years in the user-experience, design, and front-end development space, to help my clients build feature-rich and user-centered websites and digital experiences.

A few things I’m passionate about:

  • Semantic code;
  • Problem-solving through design;
  • Accessibility and web standards;
  • User-driven (and tested) feature planning, design, and development;
  • Fallout bobbleheads (sadly, I own over a dozen)

I’m currently employed as a Senior UX Designer at Velir, where I have the privilege to work with some of the world’s best-known institutions, including AARP, Brookings, Educause, The Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, The Kauffman Foundation, The Robert Wood Johnson Foundation, and Yale. I’ve also worked with a number of Fortune 500 companies, including Samsung, PepsiCo, and Unilever.

I currently live in North Carolina with my wife, son, and two cats. When I’m not shackled to my computer monitor, I enjoy exploring the area in search of the best craft beer and ramen.

Elsewhere you can find me on LinkedIn, Dribbble, and Github.
